Healthy and Yummy Vegetarian Omelette!!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • butter/olive oil
  • spices(salt & pepper)
  • milk (optional)
  • cheese(optional)
  • spinach(optional)

Instructions

  1. Beat 2 eggs into a cup
  2. Add a spoon of milk( optional),salt, pepper and mix well
  3. Heat the pan on stove
  4. Add some butter or olive oil to the pan and pour the egg mixture on the pan evenly
  5. If you wish, you can add some spinach and cheese at this point of time
  6. Wait for 2 minutes and flip the omelette until it's well done
  7. Now, take the omelette out of the pan and serve it hot with ketchup
  8. Yummy, yummy omelette is ready to eat!
  9. Enjoy!!
